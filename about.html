Tech learning centre for kids and youths

Alpha Coding is dedicated to inspire the next generation of innovators, creative thinkers, and problem solvers in the global and innovation-based economy. We offer project- and interest-based curriculum. Our 2,200-sqft facility consists of three workshops and one lounge; and is fully equipped with laptops, projectors, internet, whiteboards, and MakerBot 3D printer.
